gpsNav

The app for online GPS tracking

Have you ever wanted to install some kind to cool GPS tracking device on your website, but cannot understand any of the code for doing it? This is the app that solves all your problems concerning GPS tracking! Just call gpsNav.init() to get the ball rolling! It can also be customized very easily in a human-readable way. Just read the documentation and follow the steps to set up your own personal GPS tracking device!
